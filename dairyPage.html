Dairy Products

At Green Mart, we take pride in offering a diverse selection of dairy products sourced directly from local farms in Sri Lanka, including renowned producers like Ambewela. Our commitment to supporting local agriculture ensures that customers receive high-quality dairy products while contributing to the sustainability of rural communities.

Ambewela, nestled in the picturesque landscapes of Sri Lanka's hill country, is synonymous with excellence in dairy farming. Known for its lush pastures and meticulous farming practices, Ambewela produces premium-quality milk, cheese, yogurt, and other dairy delights. By sourcing dairy products from Ambewela and similar local farms, Green Mart not only guarantees freshness and authenticity but also promotes economic development in rural areas.

From creamy milk to artisanal cheeses, each dairy product at Green Mart reflects the rich heritage and natural abundance of Sri Lanka's agricultural heartland. Our partnership with Ambewela and other local farms underscores our commitment to providing customers with wholesome, farm-fresh dairy experiences while supporting the growth and prosperity of Sri Lanka's farming communities.
