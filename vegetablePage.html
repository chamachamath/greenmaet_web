Sunrise Farms, Nuwara Eliya

Nestled amidst the lush hills of Nuwara Eliya, Sunrise Farms basks in the cool climate and fertile soils of this scenic region. Here, amidst misty mornings and gentle breezes, farmers cultivate a bounty of fresh vegetables. From vibrant carrots to crisp lettuce, each vegetable benefits from the unique microclimate, resulting in exceptional quality and flavor. Sunrise Farms proudly supplies Green Mart with a variety of locally grown produce, harvested at the peak of perfection from the heart of Sri Lanka's hill country.

Coastal Harvest, Matara

Nestled along the sun-kissed shores of Matara in southern Sri Lanka, Coastal Harvest thrives amidst the gentle sea breezes and fertile coastal plains. Here, farmers cultivate an array of vibrant vegetables that flourish in the region's balmy climate. From juicy tomatoes to crisp cucumbers, each vegetable benefits from the proximity to the ocean, resulting in exceptional flavor and freshness. Coastal Harvest proudly partners with Green Mart, supplying a bounty of locally grown produce that reflects the bounty of Sri Lanka's coastal regions.

Highland Gardens, Horton Plains

Highland Gardens finds its home amidst the enchanting beauty of Horton Plains in central Sri Lanka. Perched at high altitudes amidst misty peaks and rolling hills, this idyllic location offers the perfect environment for cultivating a variety of highland vegetables. Here, amidst the cool mountain air and rich soil, farmers nurture crops such as carrots, broccoli, and kale, known for their exceptional quality and flavor. Highland Gardens proudly partners with Green Mart, providing customers with a taste of the pristine highlands of Sri Lanka, harvested with care and dedication from Horton Plains.
